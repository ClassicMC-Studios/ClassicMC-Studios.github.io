What I learned in this unit
In this unit about poetry I learned a lot of things.
As a reader of poetry I have learned easier ways to detect mood and tone.
Also I've learned how rhyme scheme effects the way poetry sounds and feels.
One of my biggest strengths in reading poems is understanding the meaning.
This year I have enjoyed the creation of hikaus as I've become very good at making them.
One way I have grown in writing poetry was making consistent rhyme schemes.
The most challenging form of poem that I created was a sonnet.
