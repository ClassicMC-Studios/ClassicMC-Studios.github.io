Ode to a tree by,ClassicMC
There are very few things like the tree by me
When the wind blew, the leaves would start to sing
When winter rolled around, cracked was my tree
When winter became spring,the things you would bring
But all the while there you sat, right there
And when the seasons changed and you grew old
And all the great days we spent just right there
All the things we might have done left untold
And there I would sit right where you just were
For all the age old, forever untold times
Me sitting alone with my tree, right there
But even the best days must have bedtimes
Alone as I sat just me and my tree
The one thing I'll miss the most is my tree

What this poem means
In the poem you hear about a person moving and leaving something behind
This poem is about when you leave something behind.
This poem also portrays the feeling of nostalgia

The Mountain by,ClassicMC
Atop the mountains
I watched the cities below
The wind in my face

What This poem means
In ”The Mountain” you are someone watching a city on a mountain.
The poem is about the feeling of freedom.
In this poem the person on the mountain is also implied to have no care in the world

The lost Bunker by, ClassicMC
As I walked up a mountain
I had quite a scare
For sitting right there
Was a door to nowhere
But as I drew close I noticed that
The door had been left ajar in a mudflat
And when I peeked in the things that I saw
The unexpected things in there
Caused me to run and call and leave
But as I learned more I found out that
What I saw wasn't the only one out there
As I looked around later I found even more
But now this abandoned bunker system
Shall no longer be more

What this poem means
”The Lost bunker” tells of a person stumbling on a lost bunker.
This poem is about the unknown of finding something new
This poem also shows that there's always something new to find.
