(Basis para)

Ki! Be tu Easton. Be tu gry. Be wer 5 tos mer fom. Mer kes sautee Ben ond Amber. Be wer 1 es er be kah wer veser.Be feair vou ender flacebowl er be kuh feair activitay.  Mer es sautea Dashel. Be feair mer es. Mer veser sautea Violet. Violet feairs vou ender roquet de.Mer veser kos tosh mteae.

English- Hi. I am Easton. I am fine. I have 5 family members. My parents names are Ben and amber. I have 1 brother but I don’t have a sister. I like to play baseball but I don’t like running. My brothers name is Dashel. I like my brother. My sisters name is Violet. Violet likes to play tennis. My sister is very young.
